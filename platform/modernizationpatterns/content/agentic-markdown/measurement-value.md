## Why It Matters

If value is not measured, agentic engineering will be judged by anecdotes. Measurement creates the fact base for investment, governance, prioritization, and gradual expansion of autonomy.

## Executive Questions

- What business outcomes justify the investment?
- How will we measure speed, quality, reliability, and risk?
- What evidence shows the organization is becoming more capable?
- How will we distinguish real improvement from novelty effects?
- What signals determine where to scale next?

## Key Decisions

- Select the core delivery, quality, reliability, and adoption metrics
- Define baselines and comparison periods
- Establish how to measure agent effectiveness and human oversight quality
- Map metrics to executive, delivery, and operational dashboards
- Use evidence to govern expansion, remediation, and prioritization

## Model Components

- Business value measurement
- Delivery performance
- Quality and reliability
- Risk and control health
- Adoption and proficiency
- Continuous feedback loops

## Major Activities

- Define baseline metrics before major rollout
- Create executive and operational dashboards
- Measure human and agent performance together rather than separately
- Feed results back into governance, platform, and enablement improvements
- Use value data to shape commercialization and scale-up decisions
