# Agentic Engineering Offerings

## Why offerings matter

The website should not stop at describing the model. It should show how the model becomes a service that can be bought, staffed, delivered, and expanded over time. This section translates the operating model into tangible client-facing packages.

## Recommended offering stack

### 1. Strategy Workshop
An executive and architecture-focused engagement to align on the business case, operating model, governance posture, and initial pilot roadmap.

### 2. Foundation Build
A focused engagement to stand up the platform, controls, workflows, reusable assets, and first delivery patterns required for governed agentic engineering.

### 3. Modernization Implementation
A paired delivery engagement that applies the model to a real workload, delivering software while teaching the client how to operate the method.

### 4. Managed Evolution
An ongoing service that tunes the platform, expands adoption, improves controls, and grows autonomy where evidence supports it.

## How to use this on the website

Each offering page should explain:
- who it is for
- what outcomes it drives
- what artifacts the client receives
- what phases and pillars it activates
- what a typical timeline looks like
