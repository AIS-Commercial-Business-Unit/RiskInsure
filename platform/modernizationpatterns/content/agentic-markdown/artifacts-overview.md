# Artifacts and Deliverables

## Why artifacts matter

Artifacts make the method real. They show what gets created, reviewed, approved, delivered, and reused. They also make the consulting model easier to sell because buyers can see what they actually receive.

## Core artifacts

- Operating Model Deck
- Governance Control Matrix
- Domain & Capability Map
- Agent Role Catalog
- Evidence Pack
- Operational Runbook

## Website recommendation

Add an artifacts page that groups outputs by lifecycle phase and shows:
- what the artifact is
- why it matters
- who uses it
- when it is produced
