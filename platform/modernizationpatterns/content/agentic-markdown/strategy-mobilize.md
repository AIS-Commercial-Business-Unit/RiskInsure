## Why It Matters

Enterprise modernization fails when strategy, sponsorship, governance, and delivery expectations are vague. This phase establishes the value case, decision model, and mobilization plan so agentic engineering is anchored in business priorities rather than tool enthusiasm.

## Key Decisions

- What business outcomes and modernization goals matter most?
- Which domains, products, or value streams are the right entry point?
- What governance posture and risk tiering are required?
- What operating model will govern the work?
- What capabilities must be in place before execution scales?

## Major Activities

- Define the business case and target outcomes
- Assess modernization scope and domain candidates
- Establish executive sponsorship and decision rights
- Set governance posture, policies, and autonomy boundaries
- Create rollout waves, success criteria, and mobilization roadmap
