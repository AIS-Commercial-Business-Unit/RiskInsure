## North Star

Scalable, governed agentic delivery

## Description

- This model treats agentic engineering as an enterprise operating model rather than a coding accelerator.
- It combines modernization methods, platform engineering, human oversight, and specialized agents across the full software lifecycle.
- The same model can support strategy, delivery, enablement, managed operations, and commercial packaging.

## Phases

- Strategy & Mobilize
- Design
- Build
- Release & Adopt
- Operate & Evolve

## Pillars

- Governance & Risk
- Platform & Tooling
- People & Enablement
- Human-Agent Collaboration
- Measurement & Value
