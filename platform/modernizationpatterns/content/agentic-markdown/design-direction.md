# Design direction

## Goal
Make the Agentic section look presentation-ready, executive-friendly, and commercial without changing the core information architecture.

## Desired feel
- McKinsey / Big 4 strategy deck
- modern product website
- confident, minimal, structured
- clear visual hierarchy
- high readability

## What to improve
- stronger hero sections
- tighter spacing and typography
- better use of cards and bands
- section anchors on long pages
- more intentional use of accent color
- consistent page templates across overview, pillar, and lifecycle pages

## What to avoid
- overly playful startup styling
- dense unstructured prose walls
- too many colors
- heavy animation
- redesigning the whole app before the structure is stable
