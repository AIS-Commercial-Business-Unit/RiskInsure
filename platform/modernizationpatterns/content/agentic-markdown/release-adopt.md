## Why It Matters

Release is where speed and trust are tested. This phase ensures that delivery is observable, controlled, and ready for real users without collapsing under governance or handoff friction.

## Key Decisions

- What approvals are required by release risk tier?
- What evidence is needed before promotion?
- What rollout and rollback strategy should be used?
- How will end users and client teams be onboarded?
- What signals determine whether a release is healthy?

## Major Activities

- Provision environments and validate readiness
- Run gated CI/CD and deployment workflows
- Collect and review evidence for approval
- Execute progressive rollout and rollback plans
- Train users, operators, and client teams on the release
