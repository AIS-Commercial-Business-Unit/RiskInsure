## Why It Matters

The model only becomes credible when it improves live-system performance and continuously evolves the product, platform, and governance. Operations are where long-term value and trust are proven.

## Key Decisions

- What runtime telemetry and health signals matter most?
- Which operational tasks should be agent-assisted?
- How will incidents, drift, and defects feed the backlog?
- When should autonomy be reduced, expanded, or requalified?
- How will modernization continue after the first release?

## Major Activities

- Monitor reliability, cost, compliance, and agent behavior
- Use SRE and triage agents for anomaly detection and incident support
- Feed incidents and telemetry into backlog refinement
- Refactor, remediate, and modernize based on evidence
- Requalify agents, controls, and operating procedures over time
