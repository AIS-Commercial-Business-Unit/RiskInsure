# Relationship Management - Business Requirements

**Domain**: CustomerRelationshipsMgt  
**Version**: 1.0.0  
**Date**: March 2026

---

## Business Purpose

The CustomerRelationshipsMgt domain serves as the **system of record for customer relationship identity and contact information**. It manages relationship profiles, contact details, and customer connections, providing this information to other domains throughout the insurance platform.

---

## Business Capabilities

### 1. Relationship Profile Management

**Capability**: Create and maintain relationship profiles with unique identifiers.

**Business Rules:**
- Each relationship receives a unique `RelationshipId` (CRM-{timestamp}) upon creation
- RelationshipId is generated by the API during registration
- Email address must be unique across all relationships
- Birth date is required for insurance rating and underwriting
- Zip code is required for territory-based rating

**Use Cases:**
- **New Relationship Registration**: Individual visits website and provides basic information to start quote
- **Relationship Profile Update**: Individual updates email, phone, or address information
- **Relationship Lookup**: Other domains retrieve relationship information by RelationshipId

---

### 2. Contact Information Management

**Capability**: Store and maintain relationship contact information.

**Data Captured:**
- **Email Address**: Primary communication channel (required, unique)
- **Phone Number**: Optional, for customer service contact
- **Mailing Address**: Physical address for correspondence
- **Birth Date**: Required for age-based rating
- **Zip Code**: Required for territory rating

**Business Rules:**
- Email must be valid format and unique
- Birth date must indicate individual is at least 18 years old
- Zip code must be valid US postal code (5 digits)
- Phone number follows standard US format (optional)
- Address changes are tracked with effective dates

**Communication Preferences:**
- Email preferences (marketing, transactional, policy updates)
- Preferred contact method (email, phone, mail)
- Language preference

---

### 3. Relationship Lifecycle

**Status Values:**
- **Active**: Relationship actively engaging with platform (quotes, policies)
- **Inactive**: Relationship has no active quotes or policies
- **Suspended**: Relationship access temporarily restricted
- **Closed**: Relationship account permanently closed (GDPR deletion request)

**State Transitions:**
- New → Active (upon registration)
- Active → Inactive (all policies expired, no quotes for 90+ days)
- Active → Suspended (fraud investigation, non-payment)
- Any → Closed (individual request, regulatory requirement)

## Business Events

### RelationshipCreated

**When**: Individual completes initial registration form

**Business Significance**: 
- Marks start of relationship journey
- Enables quote creation in Rating & Underwriting
- Establishes relationship identity for all downstream operations

**Key Data:**
- RelationshipId (CRM-{timestamp})
- Email address
- Birth date
- Zip code
- Registration timestamp

**Subscribers**: None (informational event)

---

### RelationshipInformationUpdated

**When**: Individual changes contact details (email, phone, address)

**Business Significance**:
- Ensures all systems have current contact information
- Triggers address verification workflows
- Updates billing and policy correspondence addresses

**Key Data:**
- RelationshipId
- Fields changed (email, phone, address)
- Old values (for audit)
- New values
- Effective date of change

**Subscribers**:
- Billing (update billing address)
- Policy (update mailing address for documents)

---

### RelationshipContactInformationChanged

**When**: Email, phone, or address modified

**Business Significance**:
- Maintains data consistency across domains
- Supports compliance with data accuracy requirements

**Key Data:**
- RelationshipId
- Contact type (email, phone, address)
- Previous value
- New value
- Change timestamp

---

### RelationshipClosed

**When**: Individual requests account closure or regulatory requirement

**Business Significance**:
- GDPR/CCPA compliance - right to be forgotten
- Personal data anonymized
- Transactional history retained for audit

**Key Data:**
- RelationshipId
- Closure reason
- Closure timestamp
- Anonymization completed flag

---

## Business Constraints

### Data Quality Requirements

1. **Email Validation**:
   - Must be valid email format
   - Must be unique (no duplicate relationships)
   - Must be verified (email confirmation link)

2. **Age Requirements**:
   - Individual must be 18+ years old to purchase insurance
   - Birth date must be reasonable (not in future, not > 120 years ago)

3. **Geographic Coverage**:
   - Currently only US zip codes supported
   - Future: Canada postal codes

### Privacy & Compliance

1. **PII Protection**:
   - Email, phone, address are Personally Identifiable Information (PII)
   - Access logged for audit purposes
   - Encryption at rest required

2. **GDPR/CCPA Compliance**:
   - Right to be forgotten (relationship deletion)
   - Right to data portability (export relationship data)
   - Consent tracking for marketing communications

3. **Data Retention**:
   - Active relationship data retained indefinitely
   - Inactive relationships: 7 years after last policy expires
   - Closed/deleted relationships: Personal data purged, transactional data anonymized

## Success Metrics

- **Relationship Registration Success Rate**: % of registrations completing successfully
- **Email Verification Rate**: % of individuals verifying email within 24 hours
- **Profile Completeness**: % of relationships with complete contact information
- **Data Accuracy**: % of email bounces, invalid addresses
- **Relationship Retention**: % of relationships with active policies or quotes
